Curry

Description

A warm and aromatic curry dish perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1 lb chicken, cubed
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, cook the chicken until golden brown.
  3. Add the curry powder and cumin, and cook for another minute.
  4. Pour in the chicken broth and coconut milk, and bring to a boil.
  5. Reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes.
  6. Meanwhile, cook the rice according to package instructions.
  7. Stir in the salt and milk, and simmer for another 5 minutes.
  8. Let the curry cool for 10 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
